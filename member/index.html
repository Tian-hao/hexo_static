Travis J. Chapa, Ph.D.

Developed a high-resolution saturation mutagenesis platform for gamma-herpesviruses and Zika virus. This genetic profiling approach combines the power of reverse genetics with next generation sequencing, allowing us to monitor thousands, even millions of mutants in parallel. This method enables quantitative characterization of many mutations that cause loss of fitness, and will significantly accelerate viral genomics, and have applications in cellular biology.
