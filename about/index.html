Graduate students

If you would like to join our laboratory, you could apply for several graduate programs at UCLA:

  • Graduate Programs in Bioscience (GPB)
    • Bioinformatics home area
    • Immunity, Microbes and Molecular Pathogenesis home area
    • Molecular Pharmacology
  • Bioengineering PhD program
  • Oral Biology PhD program
  • Medical Scientist Training Program
  • The UCLA STAR (Specialty Training and Advanced Research)

Postdoctoral fellows

We are recruiting postdocs. Please send your CV and research statement to Dr. Ren Sun (RSun@mednet.ucla.edu).

Undergraduates

If you are a UCLA undergraduate and are interested in perform research in our laboratory, you need to meet the following criteria:

  • A minimum commitment of one year.
  • A minimum of 15 hrs/week during the academic year, and full-time commitment during the summer
  • You are encouraged to receive class credit for the work in the lab by registering for one of a number of courses (i.e. Course 99 and honors thesis).
